As part of the engineering project, the nnUNet 2d model was trained on the training data of 1,251 patients. Trained models like nnUNet 3d full resolution and nnUNet 3d full resolution with data augmentation were also used. Fourth models in use is the yixnimpl-20 model

All 4 models where used in creation of diffrent ensembles(soft voting, hard voting).

3.1. Classification committees

Classification committees are a very simple method of getting a better model through combining the forecasts computed by individual models and selecting the class that had the most votes. Often, such classifier achieves greater accuracy than the best classifier in the team. This method works best when the classifiers are as independent of each other as they can get.

Voting classifiers can obtain the final result in 2 ways. By voting majority, where the number of votes is simply counted, or by soft voting, where the probabilities of belonging to the class are averaged and then class with the highest probability is selected.

3.2. Solution

Committees with majority voting and soft voting have been created in various configurations and an evaluation of these models was carried out for comparison and selection of the best model. The models will then be further analyzed and will remain conclusions drawn from the collected data
