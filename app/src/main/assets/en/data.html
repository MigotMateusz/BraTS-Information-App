2. Data

The data is provided each year by organizers of the BraTS competition. They are extensive, clinically obtained multiparameter MRI (mpMRI) scans of glioblastoma with pathologically confirmed diagnosis. Tumor sub-region data annotations are made and approved by neuroradiology experts for each patient in training, validation, and test data. Training data is shared along with annotations created by experts, while annotations for validation and test data remain confidential.

All mpMRI scans are saved as NIfTI files(.nii.gz). This type of data is special for neurological images. These images consist of 4 modalities:

  • T1(native)
  • T1Gd(with contrast)
  • T2(native)
  • FLAIR(fluid-attenuated inversion recovery)

Each of these modalities has been obtained by a different medical protocol and with various scanning devices. The segmentations were created by 1 up to 4 evaluators. Annotations include classes:

  • GD-enhancing tumor
  • Peritumoral edematous
  • Necrotic tumor core
